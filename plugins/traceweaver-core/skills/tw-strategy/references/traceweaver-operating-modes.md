`tw-strategy` operates in source-evidence mode. It may create or update
`STRATEGY.md` as grounding for target problem, approach, users, metrics, and
tracks, but it must route anything that could become authority through
`tw-requirements-review` before implementation planning or work.

Runtime, publication, remote mutation, clean replacement, package/release, and
release claims remain held.
