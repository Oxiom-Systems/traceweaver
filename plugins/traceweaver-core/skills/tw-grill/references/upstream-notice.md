TraceWeaver rewrites the workflow for its own authority model and does not copy
upstream source text into this skill. If a future change copies or substantially
adapts upstream source text, include the upstream MIT notice in the package.

evidence. They do not become implementation authority until reviewed into
`requirements.md` or an approved exception.
