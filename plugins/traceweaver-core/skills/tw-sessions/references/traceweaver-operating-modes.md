# TraceWeaver Operating Modes For tw-sessions

<!-- TRACEWEAVER: file-role=operating-mode-policy; req=REQ-TW-057; trace=TRACE-TW-037; ver=VER-TW-047 -->

`tw-sessions` operates in source-evidence mode. Session-history results and
hidden/internal helper output from `ce-session-inventory` or
`ce-session-extract` may inform analysis, but current reviewed requirements,
Intent Contract state, matrix rows, verification evidence, and publication gates
remain controlling.
